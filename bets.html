Why I build on the side

Side projects aren't about building the next unicorn. They're how I stay sharp. Each one forces me to make real decisions with no team, no budget, and no safety net — which is exactly the situation you find yourself in at a 5-person startup.

I've shipped things that didn't work. I've found things that worked better than expected. I've learned the difference between a problem worth solving and a problem worth admiring. That's the rep I'm trying to build.
