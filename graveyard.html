Museum of failures

Things that didn't make it.

Every experiment that didn't work is a decision I made, a lesson I kept, and proof that I actually ship things instead of just planning them. I'm not embarrassed by any of this.

Why this page exists

Most portfolios only show the wins. That's a distorted picture. The real signal is whether you tried things, learned from them, and kept going. I've killed more experiments than I've shipped — which means I've run a lot of experiments. Here are the ones worth memorializing.
