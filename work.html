How I work

Orientation

I find the gap and fill it

Early-stage companies have no shortage of important work. What they have a shortage of is people who can identify what that work actually is, own it without prompting, and deliver it without a lot of management overhead. That's the job I do.

Style

Low friction, high throughput

I write clear specs. I surface problems early. I make decisions when decisions need to be made. I ask for help when something matters and I haven't figured it out. I am not someone who needs a lot of process to produce a lot of work.

Signal

I ship before I've solved everything

I have three side projects and a graveyard of experiments. That's not a confession — it's a pattern. I'd rather learn from a thing I built than theorize about a thing I planned. The ratio of shipped to planned is what I'm proud of.
